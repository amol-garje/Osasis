Cloud Computing...

Cloud computing is used in various field and it is very helpfull and powerful. Cloud computing is a general term for anything that involves delivering hosted services over the internet. These services are divided into three main categories or types of cloud computing.It is on demand avilability of computer system resources ,specially the data storage and computing power,without direct management. Large clouds have functions distributed over multiple locations

What is Cloud computing ?

In server room, there should be a database server, mail server, networking, firewalls, routers, modem, switches, QPS (Query Per Second means how much queries or load will be handled by the server), configurable system, high net speed, and the maintenance engineers. To establish such IT infrastructure, we need to spend lots of money. To overcome all these problems and to reduce the IT infrastructure cost, Cloud Computing comes into existence.
